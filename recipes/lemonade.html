Lemonade

About Lemonade

Lemonade is a popular summer treat. It is a popular combination of sweet and sour. We recommend that it is served cold over ice.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Water
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Pour the sugar and lemon juice into the water
  2. Stir the mixture
  3. Add ice into the mixture
  4. Serve!
